Is your dog looking for love? Does your stud need to play? Is your dog's clock ticking? Look no further!

We are a new site that will assist your dog in need! Hound Hookup is a Seattle based (started out of a basement!) dog breeding assisting company. HH strives to connects dogs with other canines that are looking for other like companions.

Find owners across the country with certified purebred papers. All members go through a rigorous background check and have all paperwork and certification for their dog. In person or video conference are preformed to verify member's information.

Our member represent a wide variety of breeds and offer many levels of companionship, from breedig partners to play dates.
